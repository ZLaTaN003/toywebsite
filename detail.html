Enjoy a medium-dark roast with balanced, rich chocolatey softness and dark berry undertones. One of our most popular single-origin beans.

Beija Flor — meaning "flower kisser" in Portuguese — is a naturally processed Brazilian coffee delivering lush notes of hazelnut, ripe grape, and milk chocolate. Grown at high altitude in Minas Gerais by the Ribeinde family, this bean is solar-dried on raised beds for 25–30 days, developing exceptional sweetness and a velvety body.

100% single-origin Arabica coffee beans (Coffea arabica). Yellow Bourbon and Red Catuaí varieties. No additives, flavourings, or preservatives.
